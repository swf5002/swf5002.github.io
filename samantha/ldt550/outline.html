COURSE OUTLINE // bank budget save

Day 1, start with the BANK section of the site. Complete the following:


  • Read the articles about banking products.
  • Watch the video about how to write a check.
  • Watch the video about how to balance a checkbook.
  • Join the Zoom meeting to dicuss how to balance a checkbook.
  • Practice writing checks. Screen shot your practice checks and submit to trainer by 3pm.

Day 2, begin with the BUDGET section of the site.


  • Read the infographic about Financial Freedom.
  • Watch the videos in the video link.
  • Review Dave Ramsey's 7 Baby Steps
  • Notate where you land with the Baby Steps and post on the discussion board.
  • Create a flipgrid response to this question: What is one step in Dave Ramsey's 7 Baby Steps do you disagree with and why?
  • At 1pm, go to the lesson titled Creating a Monthly Budget-Flipped Classroom. Read the instrustions to the lesson on this page.
  • Create a motnly budget.
  • Review Dave Ramsey's Baby Steps under Baby Steps Review

Day 3, go to the SAVE page of the website.


  • Create a flipgrid response to this question: Based on the budget you created yesterday, how hard do you think it will be for you to save money and why?
  • What this video about saving for retirement.
  • On the discussion board, describe how you felt about saving for retirement before and after watching the video.
  • Go to Vangaurd's website and play around with their retirement calculators. Share with the class on the discussion board what your projected retirement age will be by creating an infographic on Canva.
