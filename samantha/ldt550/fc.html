BUDGET LESSON - FLIPPED CLASSROOM

AT 1PM, WE WILL BEGIN STATIONS. WE WILL SPEND 10 MINUTES AT EACH STATION, THEN SWITCH TO THE NEXT STATION IN SEQUENTIAL ORDER. SEE PARTNERS AND THE STATION YOU WILL BE STARTING WITH ON THE WHITE BOARD.

STATION 1 - VIDEO

WATCH THE VIDEO BELOW ABOUT HOW TO CREATE A BUDGET. WRITE ANY QUESTIONS YOU HAVE ON TODAY'S DISCUSSION BOARD FOR REVIEW.


STATION 2 - PRACTICING WITH A MOCK BUDGET

WHEN YOU GET TO THS STATION THERE WILL BE A PRINT OUT OF SOMEONE'S ANNUAL INCOME AND THE SUGGESTED BUDGET CATEGORIES. YOU WILL NEED TO CREATE A BUDGET FOR THIS PERSON. WHEN COMPLETED, LEAVE IN THE BLUE PAPER BIN WITH YOUR NAME.


STATION 3 - SORTING GAME

WORK WITH YOUR PARTNER TO SORT THE IMAGES OF ITEMS IN THIS SECTION INTO TWO CATEGORIES: ESSENTIAL OR NON-ESSENTIAL.


STATION 4 - CREATE CASH ENVELOPES

CREATE YOUR VERY OWN CASH ENVELOPES WITH THE SUPPLIES IN THIS SECTION!


STATION 5 - BALANCE A CHECK BOOK

USING THE PACKETS PROVIDED AT THIS SECTION, BALANCE THIS PERSON'S CHECKBOOK. LEAVE YOUR WORKSHEET IN THE YELLOW BIN WITH YOUR NAME.


STATION 6 - REFLECTIVE WRITING

IN YOUR CLASS JOURNAL, WRITE ABOUT THE CHALLENGES YOU WILL FACE WHILE TRYING TO MAINTAIN A MONHTLY BUDGET. HOW WILL YOU TRY TO OVERCOME THESE CHALLENGES?
