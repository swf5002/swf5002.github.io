  • 18. Prove 3n + 2 is even → n is even.

    a) By contraposition.
    ASSUME: 1) n is odd.
    SHOW: 3n + 2 is odd.
    2) n = 2k + 1 for some k, from 1).
    3) 3n + 2 = 3(2k + 1) + 2 = 6k + 3 + 2 = 6k + 4 + 1 = 2(3k + 2) + 1, from 2).
    4) ∴ 3n + 2 is odd.

    b) By contradiction.
    ASSUME: 1) 3n + 2 is even and 2) n is odd.
    3) 3n + 2 is odd, from 2) and steps 2)-4) in part a).
    As 1) contradicts 3), we have a contradiction.

    • i. a < b
    • ii. (a + b)/2 > a
    • iii. (a + b)/2 < b

    In order to show all three are equivalent, we show 1) i implies ii, 2) ii implies iii, and 3) iii implies i.

    1) ASSUME: a < b, then
    b > a, and adding a to both sides
    a + b > a + a, and
    a + b > 2a, and dividing both sides by 2
    (a + b)/2 > a.

    2) ASSUME: (a + b)/2 > a, then multiplying both sides by 2
    a + b > 2a, and subtracting a from both sides
    b > a, and adding b to both sides
    b + b > a + b, and
    2b > a + b, and dividing both sides by 2
    b > (a + b)/2, and
    (a + b)/2 < b.

    3) ASSUME: (a + b)/2 < b, then multiplying both sides by 2
    a + b < 2b, and subtracting b from both sides
    a < b.
