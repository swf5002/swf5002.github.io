    • 40d.

      Bezout identity for gcd(21,55)
      Euclidean determination of gcd:
      55 = 2⋅21 + 13
      21 = 1⋅13 + 8
      13 = 1⋅8 + 5
      8 = 1⋅5 + 3
      5 = 1⋅3 + 2
      3 = 1⋅2 + 1
      2 = 1⋅2 + 0
      Therefore gcd(21,55) = 1.
      Working backwards:
      1 = 3 - 1⋅2
      2 = 5 - 1⋅3 ⇒ 1 = 3 - 1(5 - 1⋅3) = 2⋅3 - 1⋅5
      3 = 8 - 1⋅5 ⇒ 1 = 2(8 - 1⋅5) - 1⋅5 = 2⋅8 - 3⋅5
      5 = 13 - 1⋅8 ⇒ 1 = 2⋅8 - 3(13 - 1⋅8) = 5⋅8 - 3⋅13
      8 = 21 - 1⋅13 ⇒ 1 = 5(21 - 1⋅13) - 3⋅13 = 5⋅21 - 8⋅13
      13 = 55 - 2⋅21 ⇒ 1 = 5⋅21 - 8(55 - 2⋅21) = 21⋅21 - 8⋅55
      Therefore, gcd(21,55) = 1 = 21⋅21 - 8⋅55 is the Bezout indentity.
