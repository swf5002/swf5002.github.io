  2. (16 points) List the first ten terms of the following sequences.


    1. the sequence obtained by starting with 10 and obtaining each term by subracting 3 from the previous term
    2. the sequence whose nth term is the sum of the first n positive integers
    3. the sequence whose nth term is 3n – 2n
    4. the sequence whose nth term is ⌊ n½ ⌋
    5. the sequence whose first two terms are 1 and 2 and each succeeding term is the sum of the two previous terms
    6. the sequence whose nth term is the largest integer whose binary expansion has n
    7. the sequence whose terms are constructed sequentially as follows: start with 1, then add 1, then multiply by 1, then add 2, then multiply by 2, and so on
    8. the sequence whose nth term is the largest integer k such that k! ≤ n
