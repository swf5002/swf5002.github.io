PROJECT on APPLICATIONS of DISCRETE MATHEMATICS

Course: IST 230
Project: 1
Deadline: See calendar on Angel for the deadline
Objective: To arrive at an understanding and appreciation of the applicability of discrete mathematics to various aspects in the field of technology.   Determine why the topic of choice is important and relevant in the field of technology.
Given: Identify applications for one of the following topics in discrete mathematics:
  • MODULE 1: Logic and Methods of Proofs
  • MODULE 2: Sets, Relations, and Functions
  • MODULE 3: Sequences, Summations, Algorithms, Integers, Matrices
  • MODULE 4: Graphs and Trees
  • MODULE 5: Grammars, Languages, Finite State Machines, Finite State Automaton
Instructions:
  1. Rank according to preference the five topics you would like to work on.   Submit your preferences by posting them in the designated Discussion Forum.   First come first serve.
  2. The instructor will determine your final topic.
Deliverable:
  1. You have the rest of the semester to conduct a thorough research on the applicability of your topic.   Applications may be in any or all of the areas of hardware, software, integration of hardware and software, management and dissemination of information, etc.   How is your discrete mathematics topic applied in the field of technology?
  2. Prepare and submit a paper (Times New Roman font, 12 pt., double-spaced lines).   Prepare to give a brief presentation (about 20 minutes) of your report in class towards the end of the semester.
